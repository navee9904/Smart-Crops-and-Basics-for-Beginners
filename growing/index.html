Essential Growing Practices

Master these fundamentals for a thriving garden.

Watering

Water deeply but infrequently to encourage deep root growth. Morning watering reduces disease. Avoid wetting leaves.

Spacing

Crowded plants compete for resources and develop disease. Follow recommended spacing for airflow and light penetration.

Mulching

A layer of organic mulch conserves moisture, suppresses weeds, and regulates soil temperature. Apply 2-4 inches around plants.
