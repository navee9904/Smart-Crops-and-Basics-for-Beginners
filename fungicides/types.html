Classification by Action

Contact Fungicides

Stay on plant surfaces and kill spores on contact. Must be applied before infection. Re-apply after rain as they wash off easily.

Systemic Fungicides

Absorbed into plant tissues. Can treat existing infections and provide longer-lasting protection. Less affected by rainfall.

Preventive

Applied before disease appears. Creates a protective barrier. Essential when conditions favor disease development.

Curative

Can stop disease after infection begins. More expensive but useful when early symptoms appear.

Rotation is Key

Alternate between fungicides with different modes of action to prevent resistance development.
