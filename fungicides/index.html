Understanding Fungal Diseases

Fungi are among the most common causes of crop loss worldwide. From powdery mildew to blight, fungal infections can devastate your plants if left unchecked.

Learn to recognize symptoms early, choose the right fungicide, and apply it safely for effective disease management.

Common Fungal Diseases

Know your enemy to protect your crops.

Powdery Mildew

White, powdery coating on leaves. Common in squash, cucumbers, and roses. Thrives in humid conditions.

Blight

Rapid browning and wilting. Affects tomatoes and potatoes. Can destroy crops within days.

Root Rot

Caused by overwatering. Plants wilt despite moist soil. Prevention is key—improve drainage.

Safety First

Always read fungicide labels carefully. Wear protective gear during application and observe pre-harvest intervals before consuming treated crops.
