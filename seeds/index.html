Why Seeds Matter

The quality of your seeds determines germination rates, plant vigor, and eventual yield. Understanding seed selection is the first step to agricultural success.

Seed quality affects germination, while crop matching ensures plants thrive in your specific climate and soil conditions.

Getting Started

New to gardening? Start with easy crops like lettuce, radishes, or beans. They grow quickly and are forgiving of beginner mistakes.
