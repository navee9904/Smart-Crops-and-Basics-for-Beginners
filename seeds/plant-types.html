Three Main Plant Categories

Most cultivated plants fall into three categories: vegetables, fruits, and field crops. Each has different growth habits, time requirements, and uses.

Understanding these helps you set realistic expectations and choose crops that match your goals.

Small balcony? Start with container vegetables. Backyard? Add a fruit tree alongside veggies for long-term rewards.
